# Contributor Profile Template
## Nexora Editorial System (v1.0)

This template defines how contributor profiles are presented across Nexora sites.

Profiles are designed to add **clarity and accountability** without implying
personal credentials, professional certification, or individual authority.

—

## Profile Display Name

Example:
- Alex Morgan
- Deniz Kaya
- Jordan Lee

Names are selected for neutrality and do not represent claims of real-world identity unless explicitly stated.

—

## Profile Description (Mandatory)

Example text:

> Alex Morgan is a contributor profile within the Nexora Editorial System.  
> This profile represents an editorial role used to organize and publish content produced under Nexora’s research and editorial processes.

This contributor does not offer personal advice or opinions.

—

## Role Label

One of the following:
- Editorial Contributor
- Research Contributor
- Analysis Contributor

No expert titles allowed.

Forbidden:
- Analyst
- Advisor
- Specialist
- Consultant
- Expert

—

## Profile Image Rules

Allowed:
- Abstract illustration
- Icon-based avatar
- Non-human symbolic graphics

Not allowed:
- Photorealistic human faces
- AI-generated “real person” portraits
- Stock photos of people

—

## Attribution Format

Articles appear as:

> By Alex Morgan  
> Editorial Contributor, Nexora Digital Labs

or

> By Nexora Editorial Desk

—

## What This Profile Represents

This profile:
- Represents a publishing role
- Ensures organizational clarity
- Improves reader navigation and trust

This profile does NOT:
- Claim professional credentials
- Represent a real individual unless stated
- Offer personalized guidance

—

## Governance & Control

All contributor profiles are:
- Created
- Managed
- Reviewed

by Nexora Digital Labs.

Profiles may be updated or retired at any time.

—

## Compliance Statement

This contributor system is designed to comply with:
- Platform transparency standards
- Consumer protection principles
- Search engine quality guidelines

—
